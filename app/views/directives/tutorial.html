First time?

Here's how to play:

  1. Find some friends.
  2. Split into two teams.
  3. Aternate players in a circle.

One the game starts, the player holding the game describes the phrase to their teammates.

This player can do anything to describe phrase, except:

  1. Say the phrase, or any part of it.
  2. Say a word that rymes with the phrase.
  3. Give any letters in the phrase.

Ony the team guesses the phrase, the game is handed to the next player. They press next and describe the next phrase.

Once the time is up, the team not holding the game wins the round.
